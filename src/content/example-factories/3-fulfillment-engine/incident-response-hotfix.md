Resolve a production incident with a targeted fix.

1. Delegate to **sre-dev** to triage and confirm impact.
2. Delegate to **service-dev**, **integration-dev**, or **data-dev** to implement the fix.
3. Delegate to **qa-dev** to test the fix.
4. Delegate to **release-manager** to deploy and verify.
5. Delegate to **sre-dev** for postmortem and follow-up.
