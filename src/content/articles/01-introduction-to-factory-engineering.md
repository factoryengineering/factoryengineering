# Introduction to Factory Engineering

Factory Engineering is a methodology for building custom software development environments by composing AI-powered tools, skills, commands, agents, and workflows.

## What is a Software Factory?

A software factory is a curated collection of development tools and processes that work together to streamline your development workflow. Think of it as an assembly line for your code, where each component has a specific role.

## Key Components

- **Skills**: Reusable capabilities that extend your AI assistant
- **Commands**: Single-purpose tools for specific tasks
- **Agents**: Autonomous components that handle complex workflows
- **Workflows**: Multi-step processes that orchestrate multiple tools

## Why Factory Engineering?

Traditional development environments require extensive manual configuration. Factory Engineering provides a composable, reproducible approach to building development environments tailored to your needs.
