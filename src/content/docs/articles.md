## Core Concepts

**Skills**: Reusable capabilities that extend your AI assistant with specialized workflows and knowledge.

**Commands**: Single-purpose tools for specific development tasks, composable and easy to use.

**Agents**: Autonomous components that handle complex workflows and decision-making processes.

**Workflows**: Multi-step processes that orchestrate multiple tools to accomplish sophisticated tasks.

## Why Factory Engineering?

Traditional development environments require extensive manual configuration. Factory Engineering provides a composable, reproducible approach to building development environments tailored to your specific needs.
